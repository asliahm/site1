Types of Exercises

Explore these different low strength exercises to keep you mobile and healthy.

  • Yoga

    Yoga enhances mobility through gentle stretching. This exercise improves flexibilty and can help you relax.

  • Walking

    As a low-impact exercise, walking is great for cardio health and increasing energy.

  • Stretching

    Stretching will help prevent injuries and decrease stiffness. It improves your range of motion too.

  • Tai Chi

    By focusing on balance and coordination, the steadiness of Tai Chi boosts mental calmnesss.

  • Water Aerobics

    Water aerobics puts minimal stress on joints but can be a full body workout.

  • Sit to Stand

    Sit to stand exercises strengthen leg and spine abilities. They are great for making everyday movements easier.

Wellness

These are simple but effective ways to take care of your mental wellbeing.

  •

    Community of People

    Partaking in social activities aids in preventing loneliness and will allow you to foster meaningful connections with others. This can lead to a better sense of belonging and offers a new source of support.

  • Nutrition

    Enjoy a delicious yet well-balanced diet filled with vitamins and minerals to increase your energy, support your health and boost your mood.

  • Start a Hobby

    Whether it's reading, art, or any other actitivty, pursuing a hobby results in joy and a sense of purpose. Finding your craft will bring about geniune passion for your choice of activity..

  • Pets

    Pets can be a source of companionship, mood improvement and a positive emotional impact. Importantly, spending time with your pet can help you reduce stress levels.

  • Play Games

    The experince of playing games lets you stay active, whether mentally or physically, and allows you to stimulate the mind. You can take a break and engage in something fun.

  • Sleep

    Getting quality sleep is important for cognitive function and a stable mood. It can reduce the risk of depression and anxiety as well!

Eating Healthy

Lets focus on food and how you can stay nutritious.

  • Essential Foods

    Review the different food groups, essential vitamins, etc and incorpate them into your daily eating habits and lifestyle.

  • Cook At Home

    Choose to cook more meals at home often than eating takeout. Gather a community and eat with loved ones.

  • Plan Your Meals

    By portioning and cooking your meals ahead of time, it becomes easier to stick to eating healthy.
